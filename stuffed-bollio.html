Stuffed Bollio

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp ADY
  • 1/2 tbsp butter
  • 2 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 1/2 top salt
  • 1 tsp sugar

Filling

  • 2 cups cream cheese
  • 1 cup chopped jalapeños
  • 1 cup shredded cheese

Bake: Temp at 450, wet the tops before baking, let rise 2x until golden brown.

Directions

Mix your dough and let it rise once. When you mix your filling, it could be the filling mentioned above, or you can make your own. Once the dough is rolled into a rectangle, put the filling lengthwise.

Then grab the two corners of the longest side and roll them onto the other two corners of the longest side, and pinch them down and tuck in the edges. Let it rest a second time until it has doubled in size, then bake.
